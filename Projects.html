FILMS

A collection of highlights...

Cold Winter Nights

Writer, Director, and Editor

One of my first short films, it follows a young apocalypse survivor who comes across a companion who speaks only Spanish. The film explores the themes of friendship through a language barrier and the human need for companionship. It was entered into a local film festival in 2019 and was nominated for Best Action Film.

Ridley Park

Writer, Director, Actor, and Editor

This film was a collaboration with my friend Townshend White; he had starred in a couple of my films before when he came to me with the idea for this one. We both co-wrote and co-directed; me focusing on direction and him focusing on writing. It follows two estranged brothers reconciling after an enormous loss all while taking a trip back to their hometown. The project took about a year to produce in its entirety and was entered into a number of film festivals.

MUSIC

Identity Crisis by Caprio

Written and recorded in a little over a year, this album was a reflection of my personal situation. I had lost friends and just gotten out of a relationship, so I felt as though I had no true identity. The album is about persevering through hardship and creating my own identity. It's available on all streaming platforms.
